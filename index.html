Understanding Hackers and Scammers

Hackers and scammers use various techniques to exploit individuals and organizations. Common scams include phishing emails, fake websites, social engineering, and tech support scams. Understanding their methods is the first step in staying safe.

How to Recognize Scams

  • Look out for urgent or threatening language.
  • Check for spelling and grammar mistakes in communications.
  • Verify the source of links before clicking.

How to Avoid Being Scammed

  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Be cautious with unsolicited emails or calls.
